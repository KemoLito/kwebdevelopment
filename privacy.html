Privacy & SMS

Last updated:

Information we collect

When you contact us via the website (forms, click-to-call, or email), we may collect your name, phone number, email, and any details you provide about your project. We use this to respond to your request and to follow up as needed.

SMS / text messaging

If you opt in to receive text messages (e.g. by checking the consent box on our forms), we may send you:

  • Confirmations and appointment reminders
  • Follow-up messages after a quote or project
  • Occasional offers or seasonal campaigns (e.g. referral discounts)

Message and data rates may apply. Frequency varies. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Reply HELP for support.

We do not sell your phone number to third parties for marketing.

Missed-call and text-back

If you call us and we miss your call, our system may send you an automated text to offer a callback. This is handled by our phone/CRM provider (e.g. GoHighLevel, Twilio, CallRail). By calling our number, you agree to potentially receive such a follow-up text. You can opt out by replying STOP.

Contact

Questions about privacy or SMS? Contact us or call us. You can also text us (opens your SMS app).
